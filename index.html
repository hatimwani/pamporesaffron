About Us

Learn More About Us

Pampore Saffron Farmers Producer Company Limited is Private incorporation also supported by government through its FPO program to help small and marginal farmers with access of better technology, credit and marketing to produce better quality commodities and for better realization of income.

  • Our Products include Saffron, Almond, Walnut, Honey, Almond oil, Apricot, Silajeet, Rajma and Kehwa powder.
    All products mentioned are of Kashmir
  • We are involved in Growing of crops, market gardening and horticulture.

Watch our Chairman's interview with PM Modi

Inauguration.

Advisor to Lieutenant Governor of J&K, Farooq Khan, visited Lethpora and inaugurated our Company under FPO Scheme being implemented by NABARD. The objective of the scheme is to provide farmers “better resouces for better realization of income”.
